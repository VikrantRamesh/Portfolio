Animetrics - Anime Analytics

Animetrics is a comprehensive data analytics project designed for Anime enthusiasts and data aficionados. It involves the extraction of data from "My Anime List" (MAL), and breaking down the data to gather exciting and beneficial information about the top anime shows out there. The project is divided into three pivotal phases: Web Scraping, Data Cleaning and analysis, and the development of a Streamlit-powered front-end website to visualize and showcase the findings.

Medistar - AI Driven Online Medical Consultancy

This project is an online platform designed to facilitate medical consultations between patients and doctors, coupled with an AI-driven self-diagnosis extending to a doctor recommendation system. It features separate dashboards for patients, doctors, and administrators, providing a seamless experience for all users involved.
